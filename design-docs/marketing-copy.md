## Steam Store Description

**ABOUT THIS GAME**

RISE ABOVE THE SUBMERSION.

NEON DRIFT is a gravity-defying action-adventure set in Neo Arcadia — a vertical megacity where the rich float above the clouds and the rest drown in darkness.

You are Kai "Drift" Vara, a rogue courier with nothing to lose and everything to prove. Master the art of Flow State — a combat-traversal system where momentum is your weapon and every surface is your playground. Wall-run. Ceiling-walk. Flip gravity itself. Chain movements into lethal ballets that dismantle the corporate machine one rooftop at a time.

The city is your playground. The fall is your weapon.

**KEY FEATURES**

- ⚡ FLOW STATE COMBAT — Seamlessly blend parkour and combat into a single, fluid system
- 🏙️ VERTICAL EXPLORATION — Five massive districts with distinct visual identity
- 🔧 GRAVITY MANIPULATION — Unlock and master gravity-defying abilities
- 💎 CORE POWER SYSTEM — Over 50 unique abilities to customize your playstyle
- 🌧️ LIVING MEGACITY — Dynamic weather, day-night cycles, reactive environments
- 👥 MULTIPLAYER MODES — Drift Race, Co-Op Heists, Arena Survival, Hunter vs Hunted
